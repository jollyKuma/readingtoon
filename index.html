ABOUT US

Who we are

ReadingToon is an interactive mobile reading application for children .That can be accessible through iStore for apple and GoogleApp for android devices.We offer basic lesson in starting to read upto the advanced level.This app is intended for the educational activity of the children aged 3 and up.

Why choose us?

We are the only educational app that take care of the need of the kids as they begins the stage of learning. We focus on the choice of the child we teach them step by step with love. We sure that the parents can trust as in nurturing their child in discovering their skills. We help teachers on their jobs and ensure them that no one in the class is behind in the lessons.
